---
title: Timeline
description: A reusable timeline for milestones, agents, Git, learning, jobs, and operational activity.
---

`Timeline` accepts caller-owned items, icons, metadata, detail content, density, and connectors. Statuses are
semantic: `completed`, `active`, `pending`, `warning`, `error`, and `pushed`.

<ComponentPreview name="timeline" />

Specialized product timelines should map their domain states onto this base rather than embedding one
product's terminology into the primitive.
